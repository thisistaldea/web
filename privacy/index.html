Your Privacy, Our Commitment

At Taldea, we deeply value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Here we explain how we handle your personal data and how you can manage it effectively.

Information Collection

We only collect your phone number and photographs for identity verification purposes, which are fundamental to offering you a personalized and secure experience at Taldea.

Use of Information

We primarily use your phone number to identify you within the platform, which is essential for maintaining a secure and reliable environment. The photographs uploaded for identity verification are used exclusively to confirm that you are who you say you are and to enhance the reliability of interactions within the community.

Photos and Privacy

All photos you upload to Taldea are private by default and will only be accessible to other users with your consent. Only in cases of reports for inappropriate content will Taldea access these photos to verify compliance with our community standards.

Sharing Information

We commit to keeping your personal information confidential and will only share it in specific circumstances that require legal compliance or are necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Taldea community.

International Transfer

We commit to keeping your personal information confidential and will only share it in specific circumstances that require legal compliance or are necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Taldea community.

International Transfer

As we operate internationally, your personal information, such as your phone number and photographs, may need to be transferred and stored on servers located in other countries. We ensure that these transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your information is adequately protected.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to improve the functionality of our platform, such as analytics providers that help us understand how our application is used. We ensure that these providers operate under strict data protection standards and with the same level of security and respect as we do.

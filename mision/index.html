Our Mision

Why

Every day, countless photos are taken, capturing moments, emotions, and memories. But there’s one thing we all wish for: to have the photos where we appear.

The truth is, many of these photos stay hidden on our friends' and family’s phones, often forgotten. Taldea exists to solve this problem — so you can recover the photos of yourself and relive those cherished moments.

How

Taldea makes sharing effortless. Using advanced facial recognition technology, the app automatically identifies you in photos taken by others and shares them with you—securely and seamlessly.

No awkward requests. No endless scrolling through group chats. Taldea works in the background to ensure you never miss a photo where you appear.

What

Taldea is more than just a photo-sharing app. It is a solution designed to ensure that you never lose a photo in which you appear. Here are its main features:

  • Automatically Share Photos

    Forget about asking your friends for photos or taking low-quality screenshots. Taldea automates the photo-sharing process, ensuring that every picture you're in reaches you directly from your contacts' phones. This means you can relive special moments without the hassle of searching for or manually downloading them.

  • Accurate and Secure Facial Recognition

    Our advanced facial recognition technology precisely identifies people in photos, ensuring that only relevant images are shared with you. The system is designed with high security and privacy standards, guaranteeing that biometric data is handled with complete confidentiality and that only you have access to photos where you appear.

  • Effortless Privacy Controls

    You decide which photos to share and with whom. Taldea offers customization options to help you manage your privacy easily. You can manually approve each photo before receiving it or allow automatic delivery from trusted contacts. Plus, you can delete shared photos at any time if you change your mind.

  • Secure and Simple Photo Sharing

    From installation to daily use, Taldea is designed to be intuitive and easy to use. You don't need to configure complex permissions or go through multiple steps to share photos. The app runs in the background, ensuring a smooth and uninterrupted experience so you can enjoy your memories effortlessly.
